Zero tolerance
Fr., developed by Sircle Networking Pvt Ltd, has a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). The safety of children is our highest priority.

1. What is prohibited

The following are strictly forbidden on Fr. and will result in immediate account termination:

  • Any content that sexually depicts or exploits a minor (CSAM)
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any attempt to gain inappropriate access to a child
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAM in any form
  • Using Fr. to facilitate exploitation or abuse of minors
  • Any communication that sexualizes or endangers children

2. How to report a concern

If you encounter content or behaviour on Fr. that you believe involves the abuse or exploitation of a child, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: Tap and hold any message, or visit a user's profile and tap "Report"
  • Email: support@cocial.in
  • NCMEC CyberTipline: missingkids.org/cybertipline
Emergency
If a child is in immediate danger, contact your local law enforcement immediately.

3. Action we take

When we become aware of CSAM or CSAE activity on Fr., we:

  • Immediately remove the content and suspend the account
  • Preserve relevant evidence
  • Report to NCMEC and cooperate fully with law enforcement

4. Child safety point of contact

Sircle Networking Pvt Ltd provides a designated point of contact who is available to respond to child safety inquiries, including from Google Play and law enforcement.
